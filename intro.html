Welcome to CilioGenics

CilioGenics is an integrated and open source, community friendly database for ciliary genes.

 CilioGenics provides a number of resources to:

  • explore the evolutionary dynamics (the comparative genomics) of each gene (20,000 protein coding genes) in the interactive heatmap.
  • explore the expression profiles of each gene in ciliated and non-ciliated cells (20,000 protein coding genes) in whole C. elegans organisms and human lung tissue (four scRNA-seq data paper).
  • view proteomics relationship between ciliary proteins and other proteins
  • search for the name of gene in each cilia related publications (over 50 cilia related publications presenting a list of ciliary genes)
  • look for cilia localization data from Protein Atlas (https://www.proteinatlas.org/)
  • search for cilia-specific motifs.

You can click on "help" button on any tab of "Gene search" page to start interactive guide.
